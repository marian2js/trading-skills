# Post Trade Review Walkthrough

Prompt:

> Use `post-trade-review` on my NVDA long where the thesis was valid, but I widened the stop after entry and turned a planned loss into a larger one.

Expected review shape:

- original thesis reconstructed before judging outcome
- setup quality separated from execution quality
- rule adherence called out explicitly
- one concrete process change, such as "do not widen stops after entry unless the plan allowed it"

This skill is static and should not grade the trade only by PnL.
