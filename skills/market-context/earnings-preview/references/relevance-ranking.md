# Relevance Ranking

The v1 ranking is deliberately simple and explainable.

## Inputs to the score

- whether the symbol is directly requested
- market-cap bucket
- whether the company is a benchmark name in a watched sector
- whether the report has broad read-through implications
- whether timing and estimate fields are present

## What the score is not

- a prediction of the stock move
- a conviction score
- a measure of trade quality

## Reporting guidance

Always pair the numeric score with a short reason, for example:

- "Mega-cap benchmark with index weight and supplier read-through."
- "Mid-cap peer read-through is useful, but broad market impact is limited."
