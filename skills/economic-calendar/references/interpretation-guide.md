# Interpretation Guide

Focus on event risk, not prediction.

## What to highlight

- high-impact releases
- clustered events that compress decision time
- events likely to affect rates, FX, index futures, or sector leadership
- missing consensus or previous values that reduce context quality

## What not to claim

- that an event will move markets in a specific direction
- that the provider has full global coverage
- that stale timestamps are harmless

## Reporting guidance

- Put source and freshness near the top.
- Distinguish scheduled events from released events.
- If timestamps or importance fields look incomplete, say so explicitly.
