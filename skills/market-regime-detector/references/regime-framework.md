# Regime Framework

The v1 framework uses four dimensions:

- trend
- volatility
- breadth
- event backdrop

## Example labels

- trending-risk-on
- trending-but-fragile
- range-bound
- defensive-risk-off
- transition

## Interpretation guidance

- Strong trend with healthy breadth and contained volatility supports trend-following tactics.
- Strong trend with narrow leadership or unstable volatility is weaker than it looks.
- Range conditions favor faster profit-taking and smaller expectations.
- Heavy event risk reduces confidence in any classification.
