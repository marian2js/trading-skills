# Sample Output

Prompt:

> Use `earnings-preview` for NVDA and semiconductors over the next two weeks. I care about the key debates and the most important read-through names.

Expected output shape:

- prioritized reports or watchlist
- why each report matters
- key pre-report debates
- read-through implications for peers or the sector
- `data_mode`: `example` or `live`
- `provider`: source used
- `retrieved_at_utc`: fetch timestamp
- `coverage_warnings` when estimates or timing fields are incomplete

Caveat:

- a well-argued preview is not a price prediction
